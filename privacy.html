Health Dashboard Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Health Dashboard is a personal application that connects to WHOOP and Hevy with your permission to display your fitness and recovery data in a private dashboard.

What data is accessed

With your authorization, the app may access data such as sleep, recovery, strain, workouts, profile information, and training history from connected services.

How data is used

Your data is used only to provide dashboard views, historical trends, and personalized health and training recommendations for your own use.

Data sharing

Your data is not sold and is not shared with third parties except as required to operate the connected services you choose to authorize.

Data storage

Data may be stored in local or private application storage controlled by the app owner for the purpose of powering the dashboard experience.

Your choices

You can stop using the app at any time and revoke access through the connected provider settings where available.
